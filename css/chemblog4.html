Sandmeyer reaction - Bromobenzene

Sr.No Compound Molecular Weight Quantity Moles Equivalent
1. Aniline 93.13 5 g 0.053 1

Preparation:

  1. Aniline (5 g, 0.053 mole) was taken in a 3 necked round bottom flask fitted with a dropping funnel and stirrer.
  2. The flask was immersed inside an ice bath.
  3. Aqueous HBr (48%, 9.3 ml) was then added drop by drop to stirred aniline at 5 OC-10 OC.
  4. Sodium Carbonate(8.445 g, 0.079 mole) and CuBr (0.5g, 0.003 mole)) were then added to the reaction mass in succession.
  5. CuBr (0.5 g, 0.003 mole)) was then added to the reaction mass at this temperature.
  6. As aqueous solution of Sodium Nitrite (7.4 g, 0.107 mole) was then added to the reaction mass at this temperature.
  7. The temperature of the reaction was allowed to warm to room temperature.
