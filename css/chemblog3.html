Williamson Ether synthesis - Diphenylether

Sr.No Compound Molecular Weight Quantity Moles Equivalent
1. Phenol 94.11 5 g 0.0531 1
2. Bromo Benzene 157.02 16.68 g 0.1062 2
3. Sodium Carbonate 105.98 8.445 g 0.079 1.5
4. CuBr 143 0.5 g 0.003

Preparation:

  1. Phenol (5 g, 0.531 mole) was taken in a 3 -necked round bottom flask (100 ml) fitted with a stirrer.
  2. Bromobenzene (16.6g, 0.1062 mole) was added to the reaction mass.
  3. The reaction mass was then stirred for 10 minutes at room temperature.
  4. Sodium Carbonate(8.445 g, 0.079 mole) and CuBr (0.5g, 0.003 mole)) were then added to the reaction mass in succession.
  5. Dean Stark apparatus was attached to the condenser that was then attached to the central neck of the round bottomed flask.
  6. The reaction was heated to 130oC (inner), and was maintained at this temperature for several hours.
  7. TLC was then taken.

Rf = Distance travelled by the reaction mass /Distance travelled by the solvent front = 2.5/5=0.5
